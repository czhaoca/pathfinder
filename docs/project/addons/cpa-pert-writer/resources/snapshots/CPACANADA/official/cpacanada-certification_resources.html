CRC: Resources for CPA practical experience requirements

Access the resources for CPA practical experience requirements.

The CPA practical experience requirements section of the Certification Resource Centre (CRC) offers valuable resources for students and candidates completing their practical experience as well as guidance materials for mentors, employers and program managers.

The General Resources section contains materials relevant to all individuals involved with the CPA practical experience requirements process. There are also sections dedicated to students/candidates, mentors and employers/program managers.
